Lesson 8: Brief Forms

Common words in English have special spellings call brief forms. These brief forms make writting NeoTaylor much faster. They should be memorized as thoroughly as the alphabet is.

Phrasing is when brief forms are written in one outline. They should generally be 3 or less strokes in size and only contain brief forms. Phrases should not use intersections.
